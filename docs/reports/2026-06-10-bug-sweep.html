Verdict
All 15 task batches landed, double-reviewed, and the final cross-cutting review returned READY.
The headline classes were exploits and dead content (free failed rocket launches, an infinite skill-point mint, 21 permanently-locked T6 nodes, unusable crystal mini-trees), persistence clock-domain bugs (satellites frozen after reload, offline drone scans lost, weather restarting every session), and a UI cluster where per-frame DOM rebuilds made buttons physically unclickable. Suite grew by 160 net passing tests; full run is ~12s after the lazy-ocean perf fix. Sixteen items are deliberately deferred with rationale in §5 — use the pickers there to dispatch a follow-up.

Highlights — the bugs that mattered most

Ten representative defects out of the 60+. Every fix cited here is commit-linked and was independently re-verified by an Opus reviewer (golden values re-derived from first principles where they changed).

H-01Failed rocket launches were free — infinite re-rollsHIGH
Bug
orbital.ts:279–307 deducted the payload, insertion package and antimatter propellant only after the success roll; SPEC §14.7 says “Fuel and the launch payload are lost.” A test even pinned the wrong behavior.
Fix
0d0e7f3 — deduction moved between validation and the roll; wrong-pin test rewritten; validation failures still cost nothing.
H-02Crystal unbind minted skill points — infinite SP exploitHIGH
Bug
Purchases charged edge costs but unbind refunded node + edge costs: bind → buy → unbind netted +7 SP, repeatable forever. Tier reset had the same asymmetry (bridges over/under-refunded; crystal SP destroyed).
Fix
69c9999, 7c03f71 — one shared charge-model walker (forEachSpCharge); reviewer-verified SP-neutral across chain+bridge+root+crystal sequences.
H-03Dead endgame content: T6 nodes, keystone gates, crystal mini-treesHIGH
Bug
All 21 depth-8 nodes were permanently unpurchasable (gate ignored the Ascendant-Core flag); keystone AND-prerequisites were bypassed by pathing (buyKeystone was dead code); bound crystals’ mini-trees were unreachable because the socket node was never owned.
Fix
05aea3b, 9d7093a, d929ba6 + anchor/branch/aura fixes (7b79136, 95e1002, 965315f).
H-04Satellites froze for a whole session after reloadHIGH
Bug
persistence.ts:901 never rebased movingTo.arrivalMs by perfShift — a sat saved mid-transit stayed “in transit” for roughly the previous session’s uptime. Buffers were also truncated to the global cap, ignoring skill-raised per-sat caps.
Fix
289eaa2, 400308e (import flow now accepts all migratable save versions too).
H-05Weather restarted from zero every page load; drones scanned nothing offlineHIGH
Bug
Every weather sample used the perf clock (resets ~0 per load) — sessions replayed identical dwells, violating §15.1; the same field was sampled incoherently (storm for losses, clear for capacity). Drone corridors flown while the tab was closed were never scanned, and doomed drones revealed their whole route before dying.
Fix
0e7d318, 3641ea5 (wall-anchored weatherClockMs, coherent biome+CO₂ field); 1a9c88c, 528f85f, b322cad (offline catch-up tick, dispatch-time fate, true polyline corridors).
H-06Merged-island land was permanently unbuildable; merges corrupted stateHIGH
Bug
placement.ts:278 validated against the primary ellipse only (§4.3 says any constituent); merges minted duplicate building ids, orphaned ocean platforms, dropped paid terrain conversions and lost absorbed storage capacity. Home-island terrain shots were no-ops until reload.
Fix
860a8b4, 90965a7–240e979, 54a6cb0 + island-qualified new ids and island-scoped consumers (7f480b2).
H-07Buttons across five panels physically couldn’t be clickedHIGH
Bug
Per-frame DOM rebuilds destroyed click targets between mousedown and mouseup: orbital Upgrade/Arm-Launch, build-queue cancel, HUD tier-reset/inventory, universe-editor biome buttons; settlement dropdowns closed while open. Any clicked panel also collapsed to a 1×1 box after a window resize.
Fix
ab8e63c–ea10626 (signature-gated renders, row caches, persistent buttons), 213657b (window-manager degenerate-layout mint).
H-08Coal furnaces served free heat forever; integrator conjured inputsMED
Bug
Heat consumers kept producing at full rate after coal hit zero (§5.1); sub-1-unit cap events were skipped, granting whole offline segments of phantom production/XP; the pass-2 supply pool ignored per-building maintenance/toxicity factors; batteries had a float-residue freeze and ignored unified cable components.
Fix
d5154ad, 611b4b2 (re-enabled the long-skipped cryo regression test), 25e1df7, 2ed73d9, 90a66b3 — golden-value changes independently re-derived by the reviewer.
H-09XP fountains from a broken cycle-time derivationHIGH
Bug
Near-zero-mass outputs collapsed derived cycle times to ~1s: the antimatter refinery ran at 1s instead of SPEC’s 10 minutes (1000 XP/s), drilling rig at 1.5s — inverting the XP leaderboard by orders of magnitude. Seven XP weights also contradicted SPEC §6 tier placements; bulk_concrete_plant consumed 2 MW and produced nothing.
Fix
0e737e3 (pins + derivation guard + cycleSecPinned flag), 1f0a0ae, 8691bac (recipe + dead-def guard test).
H-10Frame-rate-dependent discovery and instant Kessler cascadesMED
Bug
Scanner discovery and debris hits rolled per render frame — 144 Hz players discovered islands 2.4× faster than 60 Hz; a fresh debris field destroyed satellites within seconds, gutting the §14.5 “hours of observation” design.
Fix
4e62e52, 32d95b1 — dt-scaled hazards with closed-form equivalence tests, calibrated to a 1000 ms reference tick.
