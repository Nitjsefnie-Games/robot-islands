Decision
One ordered chain (T1→T5), every consumer preceded by a step introducing each prerequisite producer — end-to-end (rev2: feeder chains now explicit).
The largest structural change vs the draft: Task 0.4's concrete correction (cement + sand + stone + water → concrete) drags the heat-gated limekiln and cement_mill early, so the heat-budget concept + a coal_furnace now precede concrete instead of arriving at the steel tier. Verification surfaced two further ordering breaks the draft never had — cement_mill needs sand and concrete_plant needs fresh_water (well inserted). The scrap→steel bootstrap is taught explicitly to break the 30000-steel_beam circularity.
rev2 (human gate, OPEN #5 → INSERT): the formerly-folded single-producer feeder chains (lubricant chemistry, battery saltwater, cryo coolant) are now explicit steps inserted before their consumers. Completeness of the chlorine / calcium_sulfonate chains forced two producers beyond the human's named list — evaporator + sulfur_mine (D21). The only remaining non-step producers are exogenous (air) or diesel byproducts.
