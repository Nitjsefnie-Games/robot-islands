Action-cost SI rescale — terrain modifier · land reclamation · platform constructor

Context The SI-units rework rescaled one-time placement BOMs but left three buildings' per-operation costs as pre-SI placeholders (a flagged deferred follow-up). This spec rescales them: terrain-modifier resource-mapping to a 30-day-extraction rule (and wires its currently-unfired charge), and unifies land reclamation + platform constructor onto one per-land-tile structural basket.

Decision
Resource-mapping costs 30 days of the target's base extraction (per shot); land creation costs one shared per-tile structural basket.
Terrain-modifier rare target → ceil(baseRate/s × 30 × 86400) of that resource, charged once for the 16-tile shot (K=1). The shot charge — today computed but never deducted — gets wired for all targets (natural clears keep their existing numbers).
Land Reclamation (+1 radius) and Platform Constructor both bill tiles × LAND_TILE_COST (placeholder {steel_beam:1, concrete:10}/tile); an island's hundreds of tiles dwarf a 16-tile shot, so "platform takes more" falls out for free.
01

What's broken

Today (pre-SI placeholders)
  • Rare shot cost = 1.5 × 1 × 90 — naturalExtractionRate stubbed at 1 → flat 2160 units for EVERY vein.
  • Terrain-modifier shot cost is shown in the picker but never deducted — shots are free.
  • Reclamation = 5 × r² stone (stone-only placeholder).
  • Platform = steel ×5 / iron_ingot ×3 / wood ×10 per tile (uses steel, not steel_beam).
After
  • Rare = 30 days of the resource's real base extraction, per shot.
  • Shot charge wired + affordability-gated (picker becomes honest).
  • Reclamation = exact inscribed-tile delta × shared land basket.
  • Platform = tileCount × same land basket (steel_beam), surcharge kept.
02

Rare-target cost — 30-day rule

rareShotCost(target) = ceil( baseRatePerSec(target) × 30 × 86400 )    // units of the target resource
  baseRatePerSec(target) = extractorOutputQty(target) / extractorCycleSec(target)   // base, floor-0, no-skill
  K = 1   (cost = exactly 30 days)        charged ONCE for the 16-tile shot (not per tile)

Replaces the stubbed K(1.5)×naturalExtractionRate(=1)×PAYBACK_HORIZON(90). Uses the resource's actual base extractor cycleSec, so cost scales with extraction speed — slow resources are cheap to manufacture, fast/precious ones expensive. The anti-cheese holds: you pay in the resource's own units, so you can't fabricate what you've never discovered.

03

Wire the shot charge

The terrain-modifier shot cost (natural & rare) is computed and shown in the target picker but never deducted — shots are free today.

Wire the deduction at the single shot-fire point (main.ts onTerrainShotFire / the terrain_modifier placement-commit). Affordability-gate the shot: an unaffordable shot does not fire (no conversion, no self-destruct). On success: deduct the basket, convert, self-destruct (existing behaviour). Natural clears keep their current numbers (grass 3200 stone+1600 gear, etc.) — not re-tuned, just now actually charged, making the picker honest.

04

Unified per-land-tile basket

One shared LAND_TILE_COST — placeholder { steel_beam: 1, concrete: 10 } per land tile (tunable) — drives both land-creating buildings.

BuildingNew costReplaces
Land Reclamation Hub (+1 radius)tileDelta × LAND_TILE_COST — tileDelta = inscribed tiles at new radii − at current radii (exact ellipse geometry). Caps unchanged.5 × r² stone
Platform Constructor (new island)ceil(tileCount × LAND_TILE_COST × surcharge), tileCount ≈ π·major·minor, Volcanic/Arctic ×1.5 kept.steel×5 / iron_ingot×3 / wood×10 per tile

A Plains expansion (r 14→15) adds ≈ 88 tiles → ≈ 88 steel_beam + 880 concrete. A whole Plains island (≈ 615 tiles) → ≈ 615 steel_beam + 6150 concrete — far more than any 16-tile resource shot, so "platform takes more" needs no special rule. (steel → steel_beam is the switch the deferred note called for.)

05

Base-rate lookup

A pure helper resolves a rare target terrain → its canonical base extractor recipe (the exogenousFlow:'terrain' recipes in recipes.ts; the target→resource map already exists at terrain-modifier.ts:98-122) and returns outputQty / cycleSec. Unit-testable; feeds §02.
