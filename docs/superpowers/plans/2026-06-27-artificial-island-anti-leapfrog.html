The three gates

All pure, all in construction-gate.ts, evaluated in this order (spatial before budget) by validateArtificialPlacement.

Gate
Predicate
Kills
leapfrog-anchor
Candidate footprint must not touch the max-growth footprint of any populated island (every constituent at its BIOME_MAX_RADII caps, via the same islandsOverlap tile test merging uses).
The cheap swallow: growing an existing island one reclamation step onto a pre-positioned platform.
out-of-range
Chebyshev bbox gap to the nearest founder constituent ≤ ARTIFICIAL_RANGE_TILES = 48 (founderRangeGap).
Distant land-grabs; keeps construction local to the builder.
ratio-exceeded
Founder's lifetime artificial count (founderId-attributed, standalone + absorbed lobes) + 1 ≤ 2 × its natural constituents.
Artificial land bootstrapping itself; merging a platform away never refunds budget.
